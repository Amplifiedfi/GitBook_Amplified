**Executive Summary**

Amplified Protocol is a next-generation agentic DeFi infrastructure project creating yield-bearing synthetic assets: aiUSD, aiETH and aiBTC. These are not passive vault positions but dynamically managed on-chain assets designed to evolve how yield is generated and captured.

The protocol is powered by an AI Agent Swarm composed of Strategy Builders, Validation Agents and Execution Agents. These agentic systems operate autonomously to analyze market conditions, score yield opportunities and execute optimized strategies in real time. All actions remain within risk parameters defined by veLLT governance ensuring decentralized oversight of protocol boundaries, whitelisted platforms and model upgrades.

At launch, Amplified adopts a conservative, non-leveraged approach focused on proven yield sources:

* Dynamic concentrated liquidity provision via ALM v3, which rebalances positions on major DEXs using real-time volatility and TWAP signals to reduce impermanent loss and increase fee capture
* Strategic allocation across established on-chain lending markets
* Arbitrage execution between DEX pools as a supplementary yield stream

All agent-proposed actions go through human-in-the-loop validation during initial operations. This is a temporary safeguard to verify execution integrity. The protocol is designed to transition toward full autonomy as operational history, data quality and governance maturity increase.

For professional investors, Amplified offers:

* Continuous capital optimization through agentic management
* Transparent risk modeling with on-chain VaR, circuit breakers and emergency pause mechanisms
* On-chain audit trails for agent decisions enabling third-party risk assessment
* Fungible, composable assets that support both direct redemption and DeFi integration

The focus is not on chasing high APY through leverage or complex derivatives. It is on delivering durable, risk-adjusted returns through adaptive capital allocation backed by verifiable governance and operational safety.

Amplified represents a new class of on-chain financial product: agentic yield infrastructure built for institutional participation, transparency and long-term resilience.
